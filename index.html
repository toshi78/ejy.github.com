Welcome to Medsin Birmingham www.medsin-birmingham.org.uk

Medsin is 'the mother of all charities' in Birmingham Medschool. We get involved with the work of other charities (many of our committee are also on the committees of charities like Teddy Bear Hospital, Crossing Boarders and BWAMS) so it’s a great place to be if you like to do lots of different things! We also run our own events and campaigns: We support Student Stop AIDS and do a lot of fundraising around World AIDS Day. We support Health Poverty Action and provide elective intercalation placements through them.

MEDSIN’S NOT JUST FOR MEDICS! At Medsin we believe that everybody deserves the opportunity to be concerned about global health issues (see our vision statement for more information). We run a series of Global Health Lectures, where renowned speakers give their, often novel, angle on a world issue. Coming up in the first semester alone we’ve got a talk on health in Guantanamo Bay and on careers abroad. These talks are open to students from all courses and are very informative for politics, law, history and international relations students alike.

Visit our calendar or our 'what we do' page to find out all that Medsin’s got planned!

If you’d like to be involved in this dynamic group please contact us at medsoc-charities@contacts.bham.ac.uk
