Welcome to Medsin Birmingham www.medsin-birmingham.org.uk

Welcome to the Birmingham Medsin page, the branch here at Birmingham is an enthusiastic one with plenty to get involved with. We have several active projects and campaigns (some are listed below) and we are always looking for more! There are loads of different ways to get involved, so if you want to find out more then email us at medsoc-charities@contacts.bham.ac.uk

  • Marrow

    Birmingham Marrow has been going strong since 2000, recruiting students to the Anthony Nolan Trust Bone Marrow register; helping them take back lives from Leukaemia.

  • Crossing Borders

    Crossing Borders works with issues concerning refugees and asylum seekers, especially related to healthcare access in the Birmingham area.

  • Sexpression:UK

    Sexpression educates young people in sex and relationship issues and aim to empower them to make informed choices about their reproductive health.

  • Teddy Bear Hospital

    TBH is a society that aims to help children overcome any fears or worries they may have regarding doctors, dentists or hospitals, through role play.

  • Student Stop AIDS

    Student Stop AIDS is a place to plan World AIDS day events and future attempts to lobby the UK government and educate our peers!

  • Water4All

    The Water4All campaign works in conjunction with the organisation WaterAid by organising fundraising and awareness raising events relating to access to water and sanitation.
