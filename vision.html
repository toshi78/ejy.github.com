Medsin Birmingham Vision Statement Educate, Advocate, Act

Purpose:

  • To improve education and awareness of global health issues in the student body
  • To provide opportunities to take action to combat health inequality globally and locally
  • To be a supportive body for Medsin affiliated charities and help fundraise for them

Business:

  • To provide formal and informal education on global health issues
  • To offer leadership, guidance and support for other charities
  • To promote Medsin through publicity and events

Values:

  • That everyone should be empowered with the knowledge they require to help overcome global and local health inequalities
  • That everyone should have equality and access to a high standard of health care without bias
  • That we have the social responsibility to make this a reality
